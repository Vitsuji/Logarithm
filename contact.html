Vitsuji Aura - the Creator

Vitsuji Aura is the developer of Logarithm, who single-handedly created the idea and fought his way through the development of this project. Vitsuji is a developer who possesses a vast amount of skill and programming knowledge. While working on different projects throughout his life, ranging from software development to networking, he picked up languages which aided him in the progression towards the finilazation of his goals. He is a tenacious person who is passionate about his work and will never give up.

Logarithm was developed by Vitsuji Aura as a website as part of one of his main projects. Vitsuji has many brilliant projects stored in his mind and he especially prefered Logarithm - a place where people like him could benefit from advice and communication of delopers with the same interests. He based this social media style project on developers because he felt that even though programmers and other bright minds create software, apps, games and websites there is nothing to unite such people to form a greater future.
