Logarithm is not just a thing, it is a means of progress

Logarithm is a project developed by Vitsuji Aura in attempt to unite developers all around the globe.The Logarithm project began in the late 2016 and development was ongoing in 2017. Logarithm creates an easy and safe environment for developers to assist each other, comment on work and expand their programming knowledge and experience. Vitsuji Aura tried desperately to make Logarithm a success and hopes that it will help the programmers of the world.

Logarithm provides an opportunity for delevopers to socialize with others like them. Logarithm provides posting, chats and polls. Logarithm permits freelancing and other exchanges on site.
